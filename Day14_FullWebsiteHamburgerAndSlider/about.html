MahaLaxmi Biscuit Udhyog: The Home of Homemade Pauroti Biscuits

If you are looking for the best homemade Pauroti biscuits in Nepal, look no further than MahaLaxmi Biscuit Udhyog. We are a family-owned and operated biscuit factory located in Duhabi, a municipality in Sunsari District in the Koshi Zone of south-eastern Nepal. We have been serving our customers with love and warmth since 2003, and we are proud to be the best Pauroti factory in Duhabi.

Our biscuits are made with fresh and quality ingredients, and baked with care and passion. They are not just food, but a tradition that we uphold with pride.

    Our biscuits are:

  • Delicious and satisfying
  • Fresh and wholesome
  • Homemade and authentic
  • Affordable and convenient

We respect our customers and value their feedback. We work together as a team to deliver the best biscuits to your doorstep. Whether you enjoy them with tea, coffee, milk, or just by themselves, our biscuits will make your day brighter and happier.

Try our biscuits today and experience the taste of home in every bite!
